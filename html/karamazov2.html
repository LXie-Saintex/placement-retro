I did not do any programming exercise for the past 6 days and have made no educational mistakes that have left an impression on me. (if you exclude the project planning itself). I think I will feel the same once get to Dmitri where programming is the sole activity. By then I would feel that I'm not learning any business side of things and everything i jotted down during Ivan has to be relearnt.
