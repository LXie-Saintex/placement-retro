First week at Sun Savers. I haven't finished setting up the environment for mobile development (ios, android). Got the app running on the web okay on Wednesday and created a PR to modify README. It has been approved but not merged , mostly due to the inefficiency of CI pipelines in my opinion. It took an average of 40 mins to run the suite. When all checks have passed, the master branch often have been updated, which means I need to rebase and go through the whole process again. Also the jobs often fail due to time out rather than logical issues (when rerun it passes), which prolongs the process even longer. It is frustrating in that the tests results are indeterminate and it simply takes too long to get feedback. I think this is an area that desperately needs improvement.
